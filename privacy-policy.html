Privacy Policy

Welcome to WealthToolsHub ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our website at www.wealthtoolshub.com, how we use it, and how we protect it.

By using WealthToolsHub, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Please read it carefully before using our services.

1. Information We Collect

WealthToolsHub is a free financial calculator platform. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use our calculators.

We may automatically collect limited non-personal information when you visit our website, including:

  • Browser type and version

  • Time and date of your visit
  • Referring website address
  • IP address (anonymised)

The numbers you enter into our calculators (loan amount, interest rate, income, investments etc.) are processed entirely in your browser and are never sent to or stored on our servers.

The limited non-personal information we collect is used only to:

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising targeting, or sell it to any third parties.

3. Cookies

WealthToolsHub may use cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your browsing experience. These cookies help us understand how users interact with our website.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Analytics cookies — to understand website traffic and usage (e.g. Google Analytics)

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to use our calculators.

4. Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to collect anonymised information about how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect data such as pages visited, time on site, and traffic sources. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

For more information on how Google uses this data, visit Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites for reference purposes. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Our website is served over HTTPS (secure connection) to ensure data transmission is encrypted. As mentioned earlier, all calculator inputs are processed locally in your browser and are never transmitted to our servers.

7. Children's Privacy

WealthToolsHub is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Since WealthToolsHub does not collect personal data through calculator usage, there is no personal data to access, correct or delete. If you have any concerns about data we may hold, please contact us and we will respond promptly.
