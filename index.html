What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed payment made every month towards loan repayment. Use our free EMI calculator India to plan your finances smartly.

How EMI is Calculated?

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N – 1] Where P = Loan Amount, R = Monthly Interest Rate, N = Total Months.
