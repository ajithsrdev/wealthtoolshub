What is SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)?

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a disciplined way of investing a fixed amount in mutual funds at regular intervals such as monthly or quarterly. SIP allows investors to benefit from the power of compounding and rupee cost averaging over the long term.

How SIP Calculator Works?

Our SIP calculator estimates the future value of your investments based on monthly contribution, expected annual return, and duration. It uses the standard future value formula to calculate total wealth accumulation.

Benefits of Investing Through SIP

  • Disciplined and consistent investing
  • Power of compounding
  • Rupee cost averaging
  • Low starting investment (₹500)
  • Flexible investment amount

Is SIP Better Than Fixed Deposit?

While Fixed Deposits provide guaranteed returns, SIP investments are market-linked and have the potential to generate higher returns over long periods. SIP is suitable for long-term wealth creation goals.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is the minimum amount required for SIP?

Most mutual funds allow SIP investments starting from ₹500 per month.

2. Can I stop SIP anytime?

Yes, SIP can be stopped or modified anytime without penalties in most cases.

3. Is SIP safe?

SIP investments are subject to market risks, but they are considered safer than lump sum investing due to rupee cost averaging.

4. How long should I invest in SIP?

SIP is ideal for long-term investment goals of 5 years or more.

5. Can SIP make me a crorepati?

With disciplined investing and long-term compounding, SIP can help build substantial wealth over time.
