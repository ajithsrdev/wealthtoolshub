What is SIP?

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) allows investors to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds. It helps build wealth over time through compounding and disciplined investing.

How SIP Calculator Works?

Our SIP calculator uses the future value formula: FV = P × [((1+r)^n − 1) / r] × (1+r) Where P is monthly investment, r is monthly return rate and n is number of months.

Benefits of SIP Investment

• Power of Compounding • Rupee Cost Averaging • Flexible investment amounts • Long-term wealth creation

Is SIP Better Than FD?

SIP offers market-linked returns and has potential to generate higher long-term wealth compared to fixed deposits which provide fixed interest.
