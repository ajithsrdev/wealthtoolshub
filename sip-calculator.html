What is SIP?

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a disciplined way to invest in mutual funds. It allows investors to invest a fixed amount every month and build wealth over time through compounding.

Benefits of SIP Investment

  • Power of Compounding
  • Rupee Cost Averaging
  • Low Investment Start (₹500)
  • Flexible & Convenient

How SIP Calculator Works?

Our SIP calculator uses the future value formula to estimate total corpus based on monthly investment, expected return rate and duration.

Is SIP Better Than FD?

SIP investments are market-linked and may generate higher long-term returns compared to Fixed Deposits, which offer fixed interest rates.
