Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to messages?

We typically respond to all messages within 1–3 business days. For urgent bug reports, we try to respond within 24 hours.

Are all calculators on WealthToolsHub free?

Yes! All calculators are 100% free with no sign-up, subscription, or payment required — ever.

Can I suggest a new calculator?

Absolutely! We love suggestions from our users. Use the contact form above and select "Suggest a Calculator" as the subject.

Is my data safe when I use your calculators?

Yes. All your calculator inputs are processed entirely in your browser. We never store or transmit your financial data to our servers. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
